Welcome to The Fat Mermaid

A beachy hideaway that transports you far from the city

A world away, right here

Nestled on a quiet narrow street in Bedfordview, The Fat Mermaid is an unexpected oasis. With its whitewashed walls, blue shutters and sparkling pool, you'll feel like you've stepped onto a Greek island – without leaving Joburg.
