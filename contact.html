Frequently Asked

Quick answers about bookings.

How far in advance can I book?

We accept reservations up to 3 months in advance. For large groups, please contact us directly.

What if I'm running late?

Please call us – we'll hold your table for 15 minutes past the reserved time.

Can I modify or cancel?

Yes, reply to your confirmation email or call at least 2 hours before.

Dietary restrictions?

Absolutely – mention them in the special requests box when booking.
